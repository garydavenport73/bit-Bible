bitBible

Options

Just what you need.

This app was made to have the essentials of a Bible Study app. You can choose your reading plan, your Bible translation, and it will package it together width Easton's Bible Dictionary, The Pulpit Commentary, and a holy land map. You can save your reading progress, and download the app to your device. It will run on anything with a modern web browser.

Easton's Bible Dictionary is enhanced, and contains additional information, like family relationships and geographic locations. The Pulpit Commentary is a long academic project written over a 30 year period with over 100 authors.

📖

Online, offline, and cross-platform

The app can be used online or offline, and contains no external dependencies like images. Ideally, you can request your reading plan, Bible version, and download the app for use offline. This is in fact the intended designed, but you can also use the app as an online web app too.

The downloaded app will simply be a single html file about 50mb in size. It can be copied and shared and runs on anything with a browser. To get the bitBible app, just simply enter your reading plan and Bible Version and hit the "Get Your Bible" button.

After this, a web based app will open. In the upper navigation bar, hit the download button and directions for download will be shown. Read the instructions in order to dowload. For Desktop, Android, Linux, and Windows users, you will simply download the 50mb file.

For IPhone users, the procedure is slightly different. There is an IPhone users page you will go to, and in that page all the data is present. You simply save the page for offline use and further instructions are shown. More detailed instructions are available at this Apple Support page.
